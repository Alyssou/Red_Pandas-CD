About Red Pandas

🌿

Habitat

Red pandas live in the bamboo forests of the Himalayas and southern China, preferring cool, temperate climates.

🍃

Diet

Primarily bamboo eaters, they also enjoy fruits, acorns, roots, and occasionally eggs and small birds.

😴

Lifestyle

These nocturnal creatures spend most of their day sleeping in trees and become active during dawn and dusk.
